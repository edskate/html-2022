Plural Nouns

we form the plural of most nouns by adding s.

    1. door
    2. doors
    3. book
    4. books
we form the plural of nouns ending in s, z, ch , and x by adding es.
church (churches)
box (boxes)

Some nouns have irregular plural forms.

  • singular - Plural
  • man ---- - man
  • woman -- women
  • child ----- children
  • foot-------feet
  • tooth------teeth
  • mouse----mice
